# Real-Estate-Management-System-

Real estate involves various activities related to the buying, renting, and selling of properties, including  buildings , Mahal, and other types of real estate. 
Traditionally, this process has been time-consuming and labor-intensive, requiring personal meetings between clients and agents to verify property specifics and explore locations.
To address these challenges, a Real Estate Management System (REMS) has been proposed. 
This system leverages digital technology to offer a more efficient and user-friendly approach to real estate transactions. 
By moving property searches and bookings online, the REMS enhances convenience for users and improves operational efficiency for agents.
 The system supports a wide range of property types and uses, from residential homes to commercial spaces.
